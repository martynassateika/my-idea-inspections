Reports instances of an invalid 'ChronoUnit' passed to one of the 'plus' and 'minus' methods on JSR310 classes.

Though documented in JavaDocs, it is easy to assume a certain ChronoUnit can be added to a Temporal, when in fact it cannot. For example, adding 'ChronoUnit.WEEKS' to a 'java.time.Instant' causes a runtime exception to be thrown.
